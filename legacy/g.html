File 001 — The Method

The Science of Deduction

"You see, but you do not observe. The distinction is clear."

SH
Observation Knowledge Analysis Deduction

A Mind Like Stiletto

My brain is like an attic — I am never without the tools I need, but I arrange them with precision. Every fact is a tool; every tool must earn its place.

The common man collects data indiscriminately. I select only that which is relevant — and from that selection, the truth reveals itself.

  • 01 Observe. Note what others overlook — the mud on a shoe, the callus on a finger, the hesitation in a voice.
  • 02 Eliminate. Remove the impossible from consideration. Whatever remains, however improbable, must be the truth.
  • 03 Deduce. Chain observation to knowledge to inference. Each link must be ironclad.
  • 04 Conclude. The answer was always there — hidden in plain sight, waiting for the right mind to assemble the pieces.
