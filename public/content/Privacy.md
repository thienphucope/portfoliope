# Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to me. This policy outlines how I handle information related to your visit and usage of the "Case Archives".

## Data Collection

While this site is a personal portfolio, some features require minimal data processing to function. I process this data based on **legitimate interest** (ensuring site security and providing a functional, interactive user interface).

### 1. Technical Information
- **IP Address:** I use your IP address (formatted for privacy) as part of a temporary identifier to manage session security and backend interactions (e.g., when you edit a note). This data is temporary and typically expires after your session ends.
- **Device Information:** I detect basic device types (Mobile, Windows, etc.) from your User Agent to optimize the layout and maintain session consistency.

### 2. Interaction Data
- **Visual Effects:** The site tracks click coordinates and timing to generate temporary visual effects (like the fingerprint animation). This data is volatile and exists only for the duration of the effect.
- **Session ID:** A random, temporary session ID is generated to manage file locking and editing permissions in the Case Archives, ensuring that multiple users do not overwrite each other's work.

### 3. Archive Edits
Any content you write or edit in the "Case Archives" is processed by the backend and saved to the repository if you have the appropriate permissions.

### 4. Storage & Cookies
- **First-Party Storage:** I do not use traditional first-party tracking cookies. Instead, I use your browser's local and session storage to support site functionality, such as saving your work-in-progress drafts and maintaining your session state. This data remains on your device.
- **Third-Party Cookies (Advertising):** This website uses **Google AdSense** to serve advertisements. While I do not set tracking cookies myself, Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.
    - **Personalized Ads:** Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
    - **Opt-out:** You may opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads) or by using the consent message (CMP) provided on this site.
    - For more details, please review [Google’s Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
