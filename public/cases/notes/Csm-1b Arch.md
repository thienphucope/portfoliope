
# Conversational Speech Model Architecture

> So in short and non-scientific way, it's a text to speech model that can hear what it speaks and sticks to the context better. However, the training is extremely hard and the data is required to be diverse and conversational. There're also challenges in inference such as speech quality gradually degrading due to taking slightly bad outputs as refs.
