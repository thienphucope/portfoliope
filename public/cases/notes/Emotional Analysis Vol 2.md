## Question 1
You’re in a relationship, but you also have a female best friend. How do you handle this delicate situation?

a. "Cut off the bestie completely. Girlfriend comes first."

b. "Tell my girlfriend about her so she 'understands,' but keep hanging out and chatting like normal."

c. "Be transparent but low-key. Don't bring the friend up constantly, and if you hang out, update your girlfriend so she feels secure."

**The Play:** **Choose (c).**
**Explanation:** (a) is too extreme/simp behavior. (b) is risky—"understanding" doesn't mean she likes it. (c) is the pro move: Communication + Boundaries = Peace of mind. Never make her compete for your attention.

## Question 3
Work is slamming you, and you can't text her back instantly. What do you say?

a. "I'm busy working, be a good girl and I'll buy your favorite food later."

b. "I'm swamped, but I'll text you the second I get a break, okay? Be good!"
