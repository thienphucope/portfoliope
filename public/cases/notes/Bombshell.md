And it's called bombshell. A **bombshell body** describes a woman's physique that is considered extremely sexually attractive, curvaceous, and striking.

It typically implies an **"hourglass figure"**—characterized by a full bust and wide hips combined with a significantly narer waist. The term is derived from "blonde bombshell," a phrase popularized in the 1930s-50s Hollywood era to describe stars like Marilyn Monroe who had massive "explosive" popularity due to their alluring appearance.

**Key characteristics:**

- **Voluptuous:** Full and curvy rather than very thin.
    
- **Glamorous:** Often associated with high confidence and bold sex appeal.


The term **'bombshell'** also hints at the physical shape of an actual bomb—curvy and full. Beyond that, it describes a type of beauty that is **explosive**, capturing everyone's attention instantly, much like a bomb going off.
