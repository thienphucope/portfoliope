# Dimension Explanations

## S1
- **L:** Harsher on yourself than others; even two compliments make you want to verify authenticity first.
- **M:** Confidence fluctuates with the weather; smooth winds let you soar, headwinds make you shrink first.
- **H:** You have a general sense of yourself inwardly; not easily shaken by a stranger's words.

## S2
- **L:** Inner channels have more static; often looping in "Who am I?"
- **M:** Usually recognize yourself, but occasionally get temporarily renumbered by emotions.
- **H:** Clear on your own temper, desires, and bottom lines.

## S3
- **L:** More concerned with comfort and safety; no need to sprint life every day.
- **M:** Want to advance but also to rest; value sorting often holds internal meetings.
- **H:** Easily pushed forward by goals, growth, or some important belief.

## E1
- **L:** Emotional alarms sensitive; unread replies can lead to imagining the grand finale.
- **M:** Half trust, half probe; often inner tug-of-war in relationships.
- **H:** More willing to believe in the relationship itself; not scared off by minor disturbances.

## E2
- **L:** Emotional investment restrained; heart door not unopened, but access too strict.
- **M:** Will invest, but leave a backdoor; not all-in.
- **H:** Once committed, tends to be serious; gives emotions and energy fully.

## E3
- **L:** Easily clingy and easily clung to; temperature in relationships is important.
- **M:** Need both intimacy and independence; adjustable dependency type.
- **H:** Space sense important; even in love, need a piece of your own territory.

## A1
- **L:** Views the world with a defensive filter; suspect first, approach later.
- **M:** Neither naive nor full conspiracy; observation is instinct.
- **H:** More willing to believe in human nature and kindness; doesn't rush to sentence the world to death.

## A2
- **L:** Bend rules when possible; comfort and freedom often come first.
- **M:** Follow when needed, flexible when appropriate; not rigid.
- **H:** Strong sense of order; prefers processes over impromptu chaos.

## A3
- **L:** Low sense of meaning; many things feel like going through motions.
- **M:** Sometimes goals, sometimes slacking; life view half-booted.
- **H:** More direction in actions; knows roughly where to go.

## Ac1
- **L:** Consider avoiding failure before acting; risk aversion starts before ambition.
- **M:** Sometimes want to win, sometimes just avoid trouble; mixed motives.
- **H:** More easily ignited by results, growth, and progress sense.

## Ac2
- **L:** Tend to circle multiple times before deciding; inner meetings often overtime.
- **M:** Will think, but not to crash; normal hesitation.
- **H:** Quick to decide; once made, doesn't look back and fuss.

## Ac3
- **L:** Execution tied deeply to deadlines; later it is, more awakening-like.
- **M:** Can do, but state depends on timing; sometimes steady, sometimes slack.
- **H:** Strong drive to advance; unfinished tasks feel like a thorn in the heart.

## So1
- **L:** Social startup slow to warm; initiating needs half a day to gather energy.
- **M:** Engage if someone comes; if not, no forcing; average social elasticity.
- **H:** More willing to open the scene proactively; not afraid to show in crowds.

## So2
- **L:** Want closeness and fusion in relationships; once familiar, easily pull into inner circle.
- **M:** Want closeness but leave gaps; boundary adjusts by person.
- **H:** Strong boundaries; get too close, instinctively step back half.

## So3
- **L:** Expression direct; whatever on mind, no beating around the bush.
- **M:** Speak per atmosphere; authenticity and decorum each spared a bit.
- **H:** More proficient at self-switching per scene; authenticity layered release.
